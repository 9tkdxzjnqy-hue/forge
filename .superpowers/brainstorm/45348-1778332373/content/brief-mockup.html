The problem

Recreational UK football bettors who want to identify betting value during the World Cup have no interactive tool that combines probabilistic simulation with market odds comparison. Tipping sites are structurally broken for EV-seeking users. Read-only simulators give no market comparison. General value-betting tools use non-editable proprietary models and are not tournament-specific.

JTBD: When I'm placing bets on the World Cup and want to know if I'm getting value, I want a model I can input my own opinions into and run simulations with, so I can identify where the market price is wrong and bet with genuine conviction rather than guesswork.
